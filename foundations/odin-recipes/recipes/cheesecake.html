Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • Butter
  • Sugar (1.25 cups)
  • Crean cheese
  • Sour Cream (1 cup)
  • Vanilla (2tsp)

Constructions:

  1. Make the crust and press it into a springform pan
  2. Make the filling, the pour it over the crust
  3. Bake the cheesecake untill the filling is almost set
  4. Run a knife around the rim to loosen the sides and allow it to cool
  5. Remove the cheesecake from the pan and chill before serving
